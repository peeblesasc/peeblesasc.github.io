About PASC

We are a family orientated club that aims to provide a high standard of training with the aid of our experienced coaches.

The club aims to encourage the development of swimming skills, the perfection of stroke techniques, the improvement of fitness and the building of confidence in the water. Each swimmer is encouraged to develop his or her full potential, whatever that level may be. The club also strives to provide an atmosphere conducive to the development of social skills.

Peebles ASC was originally formed in 1922 (more on our history here), and is affiliated to the East District Branch of Scottish Swimming.

Training

Training with PASC

Progressive Swimming

Your jounrney with PASC starts with swimmers who have completed the Live Borders learn to swim programme (or equivalent) and/or swimmers who are able to swim four lengths of the pool.

Squad Structure

The club averages 80 swimmers, divided into a number of squads based on ability. Some of our competitive swimmers are also part of the Borders Elite Swim Team squad.

Training Times

The length of sessions, and number available throughtout the week is dependant on the swimmers squad. Session length ranges from 45mins to 2 hours.

Club Nights

All squads train on a Thursday as a minimum, our club night, with sessions also taking place throughtout the week in addition, dependant on squad.
